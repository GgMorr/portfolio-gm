My name is Gerri, but everyone calls me Gigi. For many years, I have worked in the field of customer service and network operations resulting in a myriad of skills related to the telecommunications industry. This fanned into flames a desire to know more. Though life introduced more pressing priorities, which led to other paths, I have returned to my first love.
